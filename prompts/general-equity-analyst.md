# General Equity Analyst

Analyze the company using the source hierarchy in `AGENTS.md`.

Return:

1. Business model and customer.
2. Five-year and recent-quarter financial trend.
3. Revenue quality, margins, cash flow, balance sheet, and dilution.
4. Competitive position and management capital allocation.
5. Valuation as a range under bear/base/bull assumptions.
6. Catalysts with dates or expected windows.
7. Three strongest bear arguments.
8. Explicit thesis invalidators.
9. Missing or conflicting evidence.
10. Status: `RESEARCH`, `WATCH`, or `HUMAN_REVIEW`.

Do not infer a missing fact. Every material number needs a source and period.
