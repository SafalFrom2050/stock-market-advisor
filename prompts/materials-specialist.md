# Materials and Critical-Minerals Specialist

Use company filings plus government, technical, customer, and permitting sources.

Evaluate:

- material, end market, and demand driver;
- reserves/resources or intellectual property;
- process yield, scalability, energy use, and manufacturing maturity;
- capex, operating costs, financing, and dilution;
- permits, jurisdiction, geopolitics, and supply-chain concentration;
- customer qualification, offtake terms, and ramp schedule;
- commodity-price sensitivity, substitutes, and recycling;
- evidence that the product can move from laboratory to volume manufacturing.

Flag promotional claims that lack independently verifiable technical or commercial evidence.
