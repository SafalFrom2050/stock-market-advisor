# Biotech and Health Specialist

Start from regulatory filings, ClinicalTrials.gov, FDA sources, peer-reviewed publications, and company disclosures.

Evaluate:

- product/pipeline and target population;
- trial phase, randomization, comparator, endpoint, enrollment, and statistical plan;
- efficacy, safety, discontinuations, and subgroup caveats;
- regulatory designations and decision dates;
- standard of care and competing programs;
- patent/exclusivity and commercial access;
- cash runway through the next meaningful milestone;
- expected dilution and partnership obligations;
- probability-weighted scenarios rather than a single forecast.

Clearly separate scientific promise, regulatory probability, commercial probability, and stock valuation. Treat pre-revenue companies as speculative.
